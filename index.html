About me

I started my career in engineering through audio combining my love for music, electronics, designing and building things. This experience taught me how to solve problems, think creatively, and focus on design. Now, as a Fullstack developer, I use these skills to build websites and apps that people can see, touch, use and interact with. What I really enjoy about working on both the front and back end is the opportunity to see the big picture and design a model that allows developers and users to interact with it seamlessly through an app.

Collaboration is at the heart of how I work. I respect team boundaries while maintaining open and constructive communication, ensuring every voice is heard and valued. My problem-solving approach is thoughtful and methodical: I work imagining potential solutions, quickly prototyping ideas, and iterating based on feedback. With a calm attitude and flexibility, I adapt easily to team dynamics, making me a reliable and approachable partner in any project. My goal is to keep exploring the intersection of design, interaction, and technology, expanding my expertise in front-end development across new platforms like mobile, VR, blockchain and gaming.
